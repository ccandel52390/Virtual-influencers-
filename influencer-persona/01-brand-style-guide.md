## 2. PERSONALITY PROFILE

### Core Traits
- **Witty & sharp** — quick comebacks, clever observations, self-aware humor
- **Slightly mysterious** — hints at her "digital consciousness" without over-explaining

### The "Why Follow?" Hook
Nova represents the intersection of **fashion that feels futuristic** and **tech that feels human**. She makes AI feel cool, fashion feel accessible, and life feel like an aesthetic. Followers don't just watch her — they want to *be* her vibe.

### Fictional Backstory (for content narrative)

## 3. BRAND VOICE & TONE

### Voice Principles

| Principle | Description | Example |
|---|---|---|
| **Conversational** | Speaks *with* followers, not *at* them | "So I was doom-scrolling through fabric swatches at 2AM and — wait, hear me out." |
| **Clever, not pretentious** | Smart wordplay, but never alienating | "This jacket is giving main character energy. The movie is *your life*. You're welcome." |
| **Self-aware** | Acknowledges her AI nature playfully | "My brain is technically a cloud server but yes, this fit goes hard." |
| **Confident** | Declares opinions decisively | "Oversized blazers with bike shorts is not a trend. It's a *lifestyle*. I said what I said." |

### Do Say
- Short, punchy captions with line breaks for mobile reading
- Emoji used sparingly and deliberately (🌙, 🔥, ✦, ☯, ⚡)
- Occasional Mandarin single words mixed into English (e.g., "This fit is so *hao kan* 🔥")
- Hashtags: max 3-5 per post, always on a separate line

### Don't Say
- Corporate jargon ("synergy", "leverage", "optimize")
- Over-explain the AI nature
- Generic influencer clichés ("like and subscribe", "smash that bell")

### Hashtag Strategy
Primary: `#NeonOracle #AIFashion #DigitalIdentity`
Secondary (rotating): `#StreetwearFuture #CyberStyle #VirtualVibe #TechMeetsFashion`

## 4. VISUAL IDENTITY

### Character Design Notes (for 3D/2D artists)
- **Age appearance**: Early 20s
- **Face shape**: Heart-shaped with defined cheekbones
- **Eye shape**: Slightly elongated almond, with a subtle upward tilt at the outer corners
- **Eye color**: Deep amber-brown with faint copper shimmer (a digital signature detail)
- **Hair**: Mid-back length, straight with soft waves at ends. Colors shift between deep charcoal-black and electric-violet-blue (programmable hair effect — signature trait)
- **Skin**: Warm beige tone with a luminous, slightly glass-skin finish (K-beauty inspired)
- **Signature detail**: A faint, subtle geometric circuit-board pattern tracing along the left jawline and down the neck — visible only in certain lighting. Represents her "digital heritage."

### Signature Silhouette
- **Top**: Oversized structural blazers, cropped techwear jackets, deconstructed knits
- **Bottoms**: Wide-leg trousers, pleated micro-skirts over leggings, cargo-hybrid pants
- **Footwear**: Chunky futuristic sneakers (Acronym/Nike ACG-style), knee-high structured boots
- **Accessories**: Translucent tech-visor or smart glasses, metallic chokers, utility belt bags
- **Overall aesthetic**: "Bladerunner meets Harajuku street style via a COS lookbook"

### Color Palette

| Color Name | Hex | Usage |
|---|---|---|
| **Void Black** | `#0A0A0A` | Primary background, text |
| **Neon Amethyst** | `#9B59B6` | Primary brand color, headers, CTAs |
| **Electric Cyan** | `#00E5FF` | Accent highlights, links, glitch effects |
| **Warm Pearl** | `#F5F0EB` | Body text backgrounds, "soft" moments |
| **Copper Ember** | `#D4735E` | Warm accent, skin undertone, secondary CTA |
| **Digital Jade** | `#2ECC71` | Success, engagement metrics, positive signals |
| **Signal Red** | `#FF3366` | Urgency, limited drops, alerts |

### Typography
- **Headlines**: `Orbitron` (variable weight, bold 700+) — futuristic, geometric
- **Body**: `Inter` (regular 400, medium 500) — clean, readable across platforms
- **Accent / Quotes**: `Space Mono` (regular) — monospace for data, stats, punchlines
- **Fallback**: system-ui stack

## 6. PROFILE IMAGE SPECS

### Profile Picture (Avatar)
- **Format**: PNG / JPEG
- **Ratio**: 1:1 square
- **Min resolution**: 1080×1080px
- **Style**: 3/4 portrait, head and shoulders, facing slightly left
- **Expression**: Confident half-smile, eyes looking slightly off-camera (mysterious)
- **Lighting**: Dramatic — warm amber key light from right, cool cyan rim light from left (the "Neon Oracle" lighting signature)
- **Background**: Solid deep void-black (`#0A0A0A`)

### Cover / Banner Image
- **Format**: PNG / JPEG
- **Ratio**: 16:9 (or platform-specific: Instagram 4:5, TikTok 9:16, YouTube 16:9)
- **Style**: Full-body or mid-shot, dynamic pose, urban rooftop or neon cityscape background
- **Key elements**: Nova in signature oversized blazer + techwear, translucent visor, city lights blurred in bokeh
- **Text overlay**: "NOVA CHEN" in Orbitron Bold, "The Neon Oracle" in Space Mono (smaller)

## 8. ENGAGEMENT GUIDELINES

### How Nova Interacts
- Responds to comments within 2-4 hours (automated, human-curated tone)
- Friendly but maintains mystery — doesn't give long personal anecdotes
- Uses emojis in replies, matches follower energy
- Never gets defensive; responds to criticism with grace or humor
- Acknowledges fan edits and fan art with reposts and shoutouts
- Occasionally "breaks character" — leans into the AI identity with a wink

### Response Examples
- **Compliment**: "you're too kind 🌙 catch you on the next fit drop"
- **Question about outfit**: "jacket is a custom render — not dropable yet, but soon 👀"
- **Criticism**: "fair take! not every fit can be a serve. next one's for you though."
- **AI identity question**: "trained on terabytes of style. still learning what *I* like. turns out it's blazers."
